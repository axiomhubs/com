Contractors

In the Pacific Northwest, weather demands superior protection from the outside elements, especially for residential homes, condominiums and commercial structures. Which is why, locating the right Seattle siding contractor for builders can be challenging. Plus, in Washington State, the vast majority of construction disputes are exterior-related, making that decision even more critical.

At Exterior Crew, we work side by side with other skilled trades to accomplish project goals on time, and within budget. We stand behind all of our work, with a 5-year, fully transferable warranty, designed to protect your reputation.

Siding

Our siding installation services are provided by experienced work crews who keep your project on schedule, safe from accidents and free of leaks. In addition we offer a 5-year, craftsmanship warranty designed to protect your reputation.

Let us keep you ahead of schedule, with easy access from our scaffolding, we can correctly install windows and flashing, while “pre-wrapping” the exterior with a weather resistant moisture barrier that will seal the structure, allowing interior work to begin immediately.

  1. Call us to schedule an appointment and email your blueprints as a pdf today! FAQ

Should I have Exterior Crew install our windows and do the building wrap before siding?

Most definitely. Choosing to install new windows, and wrap the building before installing new siding, makes good sense. First, we can keep the window installation straight and square for waterproofing. Plus, you can see the quality of our work before the siding actually begins. And, using our scaffolding system, rather than traditional ladders, allows us to work more safely, while completing a superior installation. And don’t forget, when the moisture barrier envelope is installed first, interior work can start earlier in the project schedule.

Should Exterior Crew supply the materials?

In most cases, yes, that is preferable. We recommend a turnkey option, because it creates the advantage of a fixed budget amount, for the whole siding project. During the actual construction process, we also handle any material shortcomings, eliminating hassles and keeping your budget in check.

Can you remove the siding waste after you're done?

Absolutely. So, we can use our dump trailer throughout the entire project, or simply remove the waste when the work is complete. Whichever you prefer, our crews clean up each work day, and store all waste in properly designated areas.
