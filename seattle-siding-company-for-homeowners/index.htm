Homeowners



Your home is the heartbeat for your family. The one area you hold most dear when sharing time with family, friends and loved ones. So, it is an honor and a privilege to protect your family’s residence from leaks and exterior deficiencies. We will do the job right, while respecting you and your family throughout the process.

Services

Siding

Our proven siding process, including installation, replacement and renovation services are provided by an experienced team of specialists, led by a project manager who can answer all of your questions, while providing detailed estimates and work schedules to keep you informed of what’s coming next. Our work is covered by an all-inclusive, 5-year craftsmanship warranty.

Fascia & Gutters

When replacing your siding, we also advise inspecting any roof ends for possible damage both now and in the future. If not replaced when needed, old fascia board can jeopardize new roofing with rusty, clogged gutters being a primary cause for water intrusion. To fully protect your home, include new fascia trim and gutters with your next siding project.

Windows

There is no better time to replace your aging windows, than when our scaffolding is in place and your siding has already been removed. This allows greater access for our professional crews to carefully remove the windows, while inspecting their frames for any potential damage. Once the new flashing and windows are fully installed, we complete the siding around the frame for a clean, updated look.

Exterior Painting

Your home will truly shine, when we complete the project by adding a fresh coat of paint to your newly sided residence. We help with selecting the best quality paint, designed to last for decades, including color suggestions designed to accent your home’s curb appeal, while protecting against deficiencies with our 5-year, all-inclusive warranty.

Process & Timeline

  1. Our simplified siding process is designed to make the project and delivery timelines meet your needs. We personally visit your home, providing a detailed proposal of recommendations for specific products and services.

  2. With your siding contract in place, we assign a project manager to meet with you, providing a clear plan of action and point of contact throughout the entire process. Our siding specialists then begin implementing your plan with superior quality and craftsmanship.

  3. Once your siding project and additional services are complete, we ensure your expectations have been met with a final walk through of your home. Our goal is to protect and beautify your home, one client at a time.

FAQ

How long will my siding project take?

Most projects will take between three to four weeks to finish. You are given a specific schedule before the project begins so you can assess how it works with your schedule and discuss changes with us.

How do you select materials?

Once we visit your home, our experienced team can recommend materials and creative options to achieve the look and exterior protection you want. We want to provide the best value and end result that we can achieve for you and your home. Visit our portfolio to see how we’ve used materials on homes in the Seattle area and the Eastside with different styles and architecture, but all with beautiful results.

When can you start my project?

Most installation projects begin within 30 days of signing your contract to allow us time to order materials and schedule the installation crew who will best suit for your project. We can negotiate some schedule options if a specific timeline for your project is required.

Should I replace my windows?

Most likely, yes, if your windows have not been replaced since the home was built. New windows save energy, improve heating efficiency, and reduce outside noise. Including windows with your siding replacement project now allows for the proper access to install the windows correctly while eliminating the hassle of having another exterior construction project in the future. Ask us more about this option to learn what would work best for you and your home.

Do you do repair work?

Yes, we do specialize in repair work and partial projects.
