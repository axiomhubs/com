Tenant Management



When apartments, condos, or any other commercial buildings require siding for new construction or remodeling projects, Exterior Crew will be your partner, providing commercial siding installation with the skill and project expertise needed to stay on schedule while minimizing any potential inconvenience residents may experience.

Our proven system streamlines the installation process, reducing stress by maintaining a clean and safe job site. This daily task is often overlooked, but not by us. A clean, safe job site is an important part of our service, and one that your clients or tenants will certainly appreciate. We ensure safety – for both you and us.

Services

Windows & Building Wrap

The best time to install or replace your windows is during a siding installation project. Since our professionally trained crew is already working on the building’s exterior facade, both projects can be done simultaneously, saving you time and money. This process also allows us to “pre-wrap” the building after the new windows are installed, ensuring units are protected before the siding is put into place. Let Exterior Crew install your windows and building wrap so interior work can begin faster and the project can stay ahead of schedule.

Siding

When choosing the right siding specialist, knowledge and experience are important, especially while working on commercial buildings. Whether it’s a new construction siding installation project or an existing building that needs siding replacement, we provide skilled, professional teams who deliver on tight timelines. We make safety, accessibility, and clean work areas top priorities for our clients and residents.

Exterior Painting

Let us complement your newly installed siding with a professionally applied coat of fresh paint. By handling both siding and painting services, we eliminate the need for any additional contractors or unwanted delays in the project schedule. We also offer consultations to assist in choosing the right quality paint in gorgeous designer colors, delivering a seamless, efficient, and beautiful painting solution for your project.

Fascia & Gutters

When replacing the siding on any apartments, condos, or other commercial buildings, protecting roof ends from current and future leakage is a critical component. Old fascia boards may compromise the integrity of the roofing, while rusty, clogged gutters can become a primary cause for water intrusion. The smart decision is to include new fascia trim and gutters with your next siding project to fully protect your property.

Process & Timeline

  1. Call to schedule an appointment or send us your project via email today! Our trusted approach provides you with accurate and transparent pricing and bid information, eliminating the potential for any unwanted surprises. When you are comfortable with our proposal, we will sign the contract and launch your project with a full schedule in place.

  2. Once a signed contract is in place, a dedicated project manager meets with you for an initial “pre-con” discussion. Topics will include project schedule and details relating to the job, accessibility for parking, materials storage, specified areas for equipment, and work hours designed to minimize the impact on current homeowners or tenants who occupy your units during siding replacement projects.

  3. Next, we deliver all materials, tools and equipment to your property and introduce you to our siding installation team. When the work begins, your project manager will oversee the entire process, ensuring no details are missed. Once the project is finished, we do a final walk through and make sure the job is done as agreed. We then haul away all equipment and waste within days of the project’s completion.

FAQ

When can I expect to receive a bid?

For commercial replacement projects, we need a couple of weeks to provide a detailed and accurate quote, depending on the size of the project. To ensure the quote is thorough, we visit the site twice to perform all necessary take-offs for the bid. For new construction of commercial siding installations, we coordinate with the general contractors’ schedules.

How fast can you start our project?

We usually schedule two months in advance, but can negotiate earlier start times, if needed.

Will tenants be able to safely enter or exit their homes during construction?

Yes. Our crews work hard to maintain safe and accessible job sites and this is especially true when it comes to projects with occupied units. We train our crews to utilize safety precautions that create little to no inconvenience to your tenants and we assure you that job sites are cleaned daily. Additionally to minimize interruption, our crews begin work after 8 a.m. and end at 5 p.m., unless otherwise agreed.

Should windows be replaced with siding?

Most likely, yes, if your windows have not been replaced since the units were built. New windows save energy, improve heating efficiency, and reduce outside noise. Including windows with your siding replacement project now allows for the proper access to install the windows correctly while eliminating the hassle of having another exterior construction project in the future. Ask us more about this option to learn what would work best for your project.

Do you do repair work?

Unfortunately, we do not. Our specialty is complete exterior makeover services.
