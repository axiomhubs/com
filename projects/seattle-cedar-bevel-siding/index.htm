Avenue 4 West

When the owners of this gorgeous lot in the most iconic area of Seattle, known as Queen Anne decided to custom build their luxurious new dream home, no expense was spared. The general contractor partnered with reputable and experienced siding installation specialists Exterior Crew to handle all cedar siding, board and batten, trim and millwork, including columns and custom crafted trellis.

Overlooking heart of Queen Anne

Majestic beauty overlooking the heart of Queen Anne, perched high atop a large corner lot with views of the Cascade Mountains and Puget Sound while remaining safely nestled in a gorgeously landscaped urban sanctuary.

Contemporary design with farmhouse feel

The monumental stacked stone chimney climbs skyward, amid other more contemporary styling choices, including mitered siding on all corners, detailed bend trim in corresponding colors designed to match with eaves and downspouts.

Stone and Cedar add warmth

Neatly trimmed hedges line ornate metal railings showcasing the main entrance, including beautifully detailed entry columns, pristinely wrapped beams and an inviting cappuccino-colored, stucco with matching cedar soffit.

Custom trellis with ornate railings

With views of the Cascades and Puget Sound, this residence demands space that is both functional and comfortable, like this patio featuring ornate metal railings, custom crafted columns, trellis and overhead beams.

Form falls for function

Many homes exhibit wonderful design influences, this residence captures “when form flows with function” in brilliantly clean lines, gorgeous window trims, continuous venting eaves with color coordinated downspouts and smooth honey tone natural cedar soffits.

Precision design with maximum protection

Square edges and detailed white trim work clearly sets this beautiful home apart, from tongue and groove cedar soffits with natural woodgrains, to eaves with continuous ventilation and neatly disguised downspouts in matching colors.

Impeccable styling meets exceptional siding

Rising from a sea of yellow to a luxurious residence near perfection, when incredible design and exceptional siding craftsmanship intersect amazingly beautiful spaces are created and brought to life in vivid reality.

Stacked balconies with heavenly appeal

Breathtaking double stacked balconies peer out from a garden-like, oasis of green, revealing contemporary design accents, like board and batten gables, decorative metal railings and elegant columns with detailed trim and matching cedar soffits.
