Lake Hills Signature Rebuild

This custom rebuilt Lake Hills residence overlooking Lake Sammamish in Bellevue creates a warm, ambient feeling with lots of light and sweeping waterfront vistas courtesy of stylized design cues provided by home remodeling specialist, Thomas Jacobson, in partnership with premium siding installation specialists, Exterior Crew.

Tastefully refined renovation with lake view

A feeling of warm sunny days, clear blue skies and pristine lake views embrace this newly renovated, contemporary residence overlooking Lake Sammamish.

Prestained channel siding radiates warmth

This incredibly inviting outdoor space includes modern lighting and is framed in a richly textured, prestained red western cedar channel siding with a 3 ½ inch reveal.

Modern design creates linear look

Smooth stucco paired James Hardie panels and prestained cedar channel siding create a clean, economical look, while staying true to modern design elements.

Beautiful custom mitered Cedar corners

Design sensitive paneling with gorgeous cedar channeled corners and matching round downspout reveal quality craftsmanship and an extreme attention to detail.

Richly stained Amber Cedar siding

Stunning modern design accents with quality siding construction and installation, in this case using straight nailing, with stainless steel fasteners and z-metal flashing.

Clean lines and edges

Contemporary design elements meet efficient, streamlined sustainability with gorgeous CVG cedar channel siding and design sensitive James Hardie paneling.

Design cues create unique entry

Custom design features surround this welcoming entryway, with beautiful rectangular windows, bright red front door and stained channel siding for added warmth.

Contemporary entrance with red door

Modern entry area with concrete walk, smooth stucco finished walls and stylish outdoor lighting with a bright red door, including a glorious tongue and groove cedar soffit.

Refined elegance with functional simplicity

Sloping rooflines give way to a spacious entry area, complete with earth tone cedar accents, matching rectangular windows and minimized landscaping appeal.

Traditional ranch with stylish updates

Classic design cues fade beautifully into this stylishly updated and newly renovated residence, revealing the home’s traditional ranch roots.

Accents add elegance and class

Large exposed beam overhangs provide unique architectural flavor, while matching downspouts feed a hidden drain system with metal cap flashings.

Straight roof with Cedar soffit

The cut out roofline gives the entry prominence, while adding symmetry to the matching rectangular windows and revealing the stained cedar soffit overhead.
