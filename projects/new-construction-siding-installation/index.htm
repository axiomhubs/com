New Construction Gem in Madison Park

This home in Seattle’s Madison Park area effortlessly combines modern style with a comfortable, inviting design, featuring stunning Lake Washington views and easy access to Madison Park docks and beaches. Exterior Crew was the builder’s top choice to give this new construction a flair that distinguishes it in the neighborhood. In pursuit of the modern design, we incorporated zinc interlocking panels and Artisan shiplap siding. The winning accents include a blend of mixed-grain cedar and slim brick.

Contemporary luxury home in Madison Park

This upscale innovative residence is elevated with sleek lines and subtle, sloping angles, infusing it with a sense of refinement.

The magic of mix-and-match siding

Crafting a dream house exterior with mixed grain raw cedar, slim brick in Coal Creek color, and VM Zinc interlocking panels.

An entrance that radiates style

Every day, a welcoming blend of colors and textures at the entryway greets both you and your guests.

Adding complexity with slim brick installation

When standard bricks aren't the right choice, explore the lightweight versatility of slim brick products, which demand fewer structural requirements.

Fascia enhancements: zinc wrap corner detailing

Zinc provides a modern and elegant appearance that can augment a building's visual appeal. However, it can be more expensive to install compared to some other siding materials.

Durable elegance: zinc interlocking panels

Zinc panels can be tailored to meet specific design requirements and can be used for various architectural styles. It is often a key element in achieving the objectives of modern design.

Nature’s beauty meets design aesthetics

Shiplap Artisan siding and smooth Hardie panels have been installed on the detached garage, achieving a cohesive style and long-lasting performance.

Practical benefits of vented cedar soffits

By reducing heat and moisture, vented cedar soffits can contribute to a longer-lasting roof structure. It prevents condensation and reduces the risk of mold and mildew growth in your home's structural components.

Fail-safe color combinations for modern designs

Wood, grey, and black, complemented by white elements, form a foolproof palette for crafting a contemporary and innovative designs.

Discover the nuances of our expert installation

On the house's south wall, our team installed 9" exposed Shiplap Artisan siding. The addition of aluminum flashing and coping brought everything together.
