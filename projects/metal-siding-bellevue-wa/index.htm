Gorgeous deck with enclosed design

The deck is wrapped with Nu-Ray mechanically seamed, metal siding for optimal panel performance and weather resistance, while protecting and maintaining the unique design features of the structure.

Cedar soffits & stone accents

Custom installed cedar soffits using channel siding provide both warmth and functionality, while the overlapping boards and enclosed deck design help protect against moisture and the outdoor elements.

Cedar accent creates clean appearance

Stunning combination of material, design, and craftsmanship, with the cedar channel siding blending perfectly with the Nu-Ray Metal 2000 panels and matching stacked stone accent walls.

Channel metal trim completes windows

Beautifully installed standing metal siding requires trim on both the wall and all windows, in this case, an L metal outside corner with J channel metal starters for a finished look.

Matching glass with metal design

Gorgeous angled glass windows and Nu-Ray (WD-2) standing seam metal siding showcase a beautifully enclosed deck with cedar soffits and stunning stone wrap.

Metal siding & round downspout

Standing metal siding creates simple, clean lines, including round downspouts for excellent drainage and a concealed fastener with floating clip system that allows for thermal expansion and contraction.

Multiple siding types blend perfectly

Quality siding installations offer superior materials with options designed to meet the requirements of any build, including the use of design sensitive HardiePanel, Nu-Ray Metal siding, and cedar channel siding.

Smooth siding panel designs

Selecting the right siding is critical for any project, James Hardie infuses each panel with quality, performance, and protection, installed here using X, H, and Z-metal and round downspouts to compliment the design.

Amazing design with siding materials

Inviting transition from indoor area to spacious outdoor deck with large windows, sliding glass doors, and a subtle shift from Nu-Ray metal siding to beautiful cedar accents.

Pop outs with metal soffits

Attractive Nu-Ray standing seam siding enhances both pop outs, complete with matching metal soffits, recessed lighting, and unique gutter design to match railing.

Side view custom siding panels

Custom designed and professionally installed James Hardie siding panels, with Nu-Ray standing metal siding, and matching stone accent wrap create a stunning visual, while providing maximum moisture protection.

Caulking application & seam construction

Superior quality depends on expert craftsmanship combined with high performance materials like James Hardie panels seamed together with Nu-Ray Metal siding using industry leading Quad Max OSI caulking sealant.
